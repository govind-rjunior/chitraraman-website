About Chitra Raman

Chitra Raman, an educational professional and HPCL (historical, cultural, and literature) storyteller, crafts one-of-a-kind handmade dolls and soft-toy depictions that bring to life the rich tapestry of Vaishnavite heritage. Having trained under Smt. Revathi, she translates the epics and pasurams into tactile experiences that engage audiences of all ages.

Based in India, Chitra teaches workshops on doll-making, conducts storytelling sessions, creates festival tableaux, and designs rangoli art displays. Her creations depict the 30 Tiruppavai pasurams, the 11 Varanam Ayiram pasurams, the lives of the Alwars, and scenes from Divya Desams and traditional festivals.
